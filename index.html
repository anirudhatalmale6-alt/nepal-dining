Our Story

A Taste of the Himalayas in Hokkaido

Nestled in the heart of Nakafurano, surrounded by Furano's breathtaking lavender fields, rolling farmlands, and picturesque mountain landscapes, Nepal Dining brings the authentic flavors of Nepal and India together with the warmth of South Asian hospitality to Japan's northern countryside.

Every dish is carefully prepared using traditional recipes and cooking techniques. We combine hand-ground spices imported from Nepal and India with fresh vegetables, premium dairy products, and the renowned Nanatsuboshi rice sourced from Furano and the surrounding region, creating meals that are rich in flavor, freshness, and quality.

Furano is celebrated for its fertile land, clean environment, and exceptional agricultural produce. The region's fresh seasonal vegetables, locally produced dairy, and high-quality rice add a unique character to our cuisine, allowing us to blend the finest local ingredients with the vibrant culinary traditions of Nepal and India.

Our freshly baked naan, tandoori specialties, and aromatic curries, prepared daily in our traditional tandoor oven, take you on a flavorful journey through the lively streets of New Delhi, the cultural heart of Kathmandu, and the rich culinary heritage of the Himalayas.

We are proud to be one of the highest-rated restaurants in the Furano–Nakafurano area. The love and support of local Japanese families, visitors exploring Furano, and travelers from around the world have made Nepal Dining a truly special destination.

We warmly invite you to experience the perfect harmony of Furano's fresh local produce and the authentic tastes of Nepal and India at Nepal Dining.
