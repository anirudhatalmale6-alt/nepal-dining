Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer halal food?
Yes! Our kitchen follows halal preparation guidelines. Please inform our staff of any dietary requirements when ordering.
Is English spoken at the restaurant?
Absolutely. Our staff speaks English and we offer English menus. We welcome international guests warmly.
Do I need a reservation?
Walk-ins are welcome but reservations are highly recommended, especially during Furano's lavender season (June–August).
Do you offer takeout?
Yes! Takeout orders are available. Please call ahead or use our online ordering system.
